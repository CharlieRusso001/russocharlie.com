Participate and Earn Points

Our point system rewards members for active engagement, participation in events, volunteering, and supporting chapter initiatives. Members can earn recognition like "Member of the Month" or "Member of the Semester." During the spring semester, the two members with the highest points get a fully funded opportunity to attend the International Collegiate Conference (ICC) by FAU AMA. At the ICC, you can expand your professional network, participate in competitions, gain industry insights, and represent our chapter.
